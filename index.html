Delicious Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • Chocolate frosting (for topping)
  • Chocolate shavings (for garnish)
  • Fresh strawberries (for garnish)
  • A sprinkle of powdered sugar (for decoration)

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer.
  5. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin).
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool for 10 minutes in the pans, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.
  9. Frost with chocolate frosting and decorate with chocolate shavings and fresh strawberries.
  10. Slice and serve your delicious chocolate cake!
